Steps

  1. Cook the rice in a pan of boiling salted water according to the packet instructions, the drain and refresh under cold water, leaving it in the colander
  2. At the same time, poach the fish in a large paf of simmering salted water on a medium heat for 10 minutes, then carefully remove with a slotten spoon. Turn the heat up under the poaching water and bring back to a good simmer.
  3. Meanwhile, peel the onions and finely slice with the red chilli (deseed if you like) and coriander stalks. Peel nad finely chop the garling and ginger.
